Understanding Loan Risk Prediction

This AI-powered Loan Risk Predictor evaluates your loan application by analyzing key features that influence creditworthiness. The model helps banks and lenders estimate the risk of default.

Key Features Used in Prediction:

  • Credit History: Past record of loan repayments.
  • Applicant Income: Monthly income of the applicant.
  • Loan Amount: Amount requested as loan.
  • Coapplicant Income: Income of coapplicant (if any).
  • Loan Amount Term: Duration in months to repay loan.
  • Property Area: Location of property (Urban, Rural, etc.).
  • Dependents: Number of dependents supported.
  • Marital Status: Whether married or not.
  • Education: Graduate or not.
  • Self Employed: If self-employed or salaried.
  • Gender: Applicant's gender.

The model uses these features to classify the loan application into High Risk or Low Risk. This helps in faster and more accurate loan processing.
